Who we are?

Elseian is built by two graduates who are rebels in the Indian Education System. This system is unfair and inadequate and is not capable of providing the required education for students to achieve their aspirations. Most of what we learned in college was bookish jargon which is not applicable in the real world and that is what ‘elseian’ desires to change.

To empower every student to get access to great learning experiences, amazing friends and opportunities.
